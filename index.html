Run sandboxed, native applications from your browser with the click of a link.

Quick & Easy

Click on a link, and Toothless will download, install, and start an application for you. With Toothless apps, there's no need to manually go through this rigamorole any longer.

More Secure

Toothless runs applications in Docker containers, which separate them from the rest of your operating system. This means you can worry less about trusting your applications, and spend more time using them.

Full Control

Toothless comes with a control panel from which you can start, stop, and uninstall applications you've used, all with a single click.
